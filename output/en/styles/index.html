The 17 Faces of Travel

Every travel style is a different operating model for moving through the world. TourVsTravel treats each style as a real decision path shaped by cost, autonomy, coordination burden, predictability, depth, and relationship to place.

Decision architecture

Why styles matter

The same destination can produce radically different experiences depending on the travel form. The styles index turns that difference into a navigable system.

Constraint fit How well the style survives real limits.
Operational burden How much complexity the traveler must absorb.
Experience depth How directly the traveler encounters place.
