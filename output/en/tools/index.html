Decision sequence

How the tools work together

The tools are arranged as a progression: understand the styles, compare their behavior, then narrow the match according to constraints and intent.

Step 1 Identify the travel form.
Step 2 Compare constraints and tradeoffs.
Step 3 Move toward fit, not generic preference.

Next action

Begin with the comparison layer

The strongest first step is to compare travel forms before comparing destinations. The same place can produce very different outcomes depending on the structure of travel.
