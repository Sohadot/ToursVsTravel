Formal definition

This format is defined by intentional closeness to local life rather than by standard tourism distance.

This page defines the travel form as a decision structure, not as a promotional category.

Boundary of the type

A reference system needs boundaries. The goal is to define what belongs inside this travel form and what does not.

Included in this type

  • Homestays
  • Cultural exchange stays
  • Locally embedded experiential accommodation

Excluded from this type

  • Standard hotel-first tourism
  • Surface-level cultural sightseeing without local embeddedness

Structural strengths

  • High potential for lived local texture.
  • Stronger cultural contact than accommodation-only tourism.
  • Can generate deeper memory value than surface sightseeing.

Structural weaknesses

  • Comfort, privacy, or predictability may be lower.
  • Fit depends strongly on host quality and traveler expectations.
  • Not all travelers want relational intensity.

Fit boundaries

Best fit for

  • Travelers seeking real cultural proximity.
  • Users who value depth over polished standardization.

Poor fit for

  • Travelers who require high privacy or uniform comfort.
  • Users uncomfortable with relational or contextual unpredictability.
