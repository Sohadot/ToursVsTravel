Répondez aux six questions

0 question sur 6 répondue

01How much structure do you want?

Structure determines whether the trip feels planned, flexible, or fully self-directed.

02How much autonomy do you want?

Autonomy measures how much control you want over timing, movement, and decisions.

03How much support do you need?

Support includes logistics, safety, translation, local access, and problem-solving.

04How much complexity can you manage?

Some styles require planning, adaptation, uncertainty, and operational effort.

05How deep do you want the experience to be?

Depth measures whether the trip is primarily comfortable, balanced, or immersive.

06How predictable should the trip feel?

Predictability affects comfort, risk, flexibility, and emotional load.
