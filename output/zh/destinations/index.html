参考论点

目的地不只是你去哪里

目的地是访问、节奏、成本、摩擦、支持、暴露度和体验深度的系统。

Layer 1 Place

The physical destination: city, coast, route, region, or landscape.

Layer 2 Travel form

The method used to enter, move through, and experience that place.

Layer 3 Traveler fit

The relationship between the place, the style, and the traveler’s constraints.

解释镜头

目的地如何改变

在建立单独目的地页面前，TourVsTravel 先定义系统化解释镜头。

01 Access

How the place is entered

A destination changes when access is handled by a guide, a cruise route, a rental car, public transport, or self-navigation.

Access is the first layer of interpretation.
02 Rhythm

How time moves there

The same city can feel compressed, slow, curated, chaotic, luxurious, or improvisational depending on the pace of travel.

Pace changes perception.
03 Friction

How much difficulty is exposed

Some styles hide friction; others make it part of the experience. Both produce different versions of the same place.

Friction is not always failure.
04 Support

How much mediation exists

Translation, local knowledge, safety, and logistics can make the same destination feel controlled or open-ended.

Support changes confidence.
05 Depth

How directly the place is encountered

A destination may be consumed through highlights, lived through routine, or explored through immersion.

Depth changes meaning.
06 Cost logic

How value is distributed

Luxury, budget, package, slow, and independent travel distribute cost across comfort, time, access, and autonomy differently.

Cost is a structure, not just a price.
